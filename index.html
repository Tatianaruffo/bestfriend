About us

Since 1999, we've invested $300 million in lifesaving animal welfare work across the country. With more than 4,000 animal welfare partners, we inspire and empower communities to make a difference in the lives of animals. Through our adoption efforts, we've brought more than 6.5 million pets together with loving families, And we're just getting started. Our adoption center is more than just a great place to find and adopt the perfect pet. Friendly animal experts at each location offer services and support for every stage of an animal’s life. Plus, we work with individuals and organizations across Ireland and beyond, advocating for the health and safety of animals, no matter where they are.

Mission To engage the hearts, hands, and minds of the community to help animals. Vision To compassionately and responsibly create a more humane world for animals. Core Values Be good to animals. Partner with people. Lead responsibly with compassion.
